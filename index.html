Lo Pasado De Moda Hace Tendencia

Démodé artists—with a multi-ethnic proposition that aims towards integrating the world’s sounds and aesthetics, would like to record a video-clip, in which richness of cultural fusions can be manifested. Our project, which is made up of artists with various nationalities, includes divergent traditions such as: Mediterranean, South-American and American. In this way we wish to show, throught music and audiovisual language, that art is composed of peace, development and coexistance.
